ODOO PARTNER APPROVAL

Partner Approval module is made to allow you to approve or reject contacts only by the contact manager. It also triggers or enables easy search or filter contact by state including Draft, Under Approval, Approved or Not Approved. It’s totally up to the managers whether they’re going to give approval, leave it under process, keep it under hold, or reject it.

Odoo Functional operation

  • Under the Draft state, a normal contact shown in the draft after being created, it moves to 'Under Approval' state by default.
  • The Under Approval state indicates that the contact is still not approved by the contact manager. Once it’s approved, the contact can use the whole Odoo which is impossible without the approval.
  • This app will allow the contact manager to have access over multiple contacts at once.
  • Publish or Unpublish Products to WooCommerce Store from Odoo
  • No special configuration is required during the installation in order to minimize the installation hassles.

All Backdate and Remarks for Stock Transfer, Receipt, Delivery, Inventory Adjustment, Manufacturing and Scrap Order Odoo App

All Stock transfer Backdate and Remarks Odoo apps help to put custom backdate and remarks on all kind of Stock Movement and Transfer i.e Receipt, Delivery, Internal stock transfer,Inventory Adjustment, Manufacturing and Scrap Order. This odoo modules allow warehouse backdating operations in Odoo warehouse for Receipt, Delivery, Internal stock transfer,Inventory Adjustment, Manufacturing and Scrap Order. Assigned Backdate will be transfer to stock entries and accounting entries automatically when you deliver, received, transfer, manufacturing and scrap the products for warehouse to any kind of stock movement. Whatever remarks you want to add you can add those custom remarks on Receipt, Delivery, Internal stock transfer,Inventory Adjustment, Manufacturing and Scrap Order and this remarks automatically pass on stock accounting journal entry if you set inventory valuation method as Automated.

Who are we?

We are a bunch of geeks who love to solve problems with the help of new technology. We have a long history with Odoo. We are working on Odoo since it was called TinyERP or OpenERP. We worked on several Odoo versions starting from Odoo v5. So we saw the growth of Odoo from bottom to up. Our expertise with Odoo helps us serve a wide range of Odoo services under a single roof.

Meet Our Key Team Members

We believe in a team that can carry a big punch. As our happy clients already know, our team is capable of delivering big results. we are a talented and dedicated team focused on delivering a real result for our clients.

Parth Gajjar
Founder, MD

Parth Gajjar is working with Odoo since version 6. He worked in Odoo's R&D department for 7 years and developed several key features. He also authored Odoo 12 development cookbook and Odoo 14 development cookbook. He loves to imagine new things in odoo and bring them to reality.
